IT STARTS WITH US.

Mateu is a Mediterranean looking Spanish actor living in the United States. From a young age, he displayed a natural talent for the performing arts and athletics, which further honed his physical abilities and discipline. Mateu is an accomplished actor, producer, theater enthusiast, and casting expert who has made his mark in the world of entertainment.

Challenging the status quo, driving forward, never backing down. The future of intelligence. The future of excellence. The future of defiance, innovation, and sport. This is where it all begins.



Mateu began honing his skills in various local theater productions before embarking on a professional career in the world of acting, starting with musical theater and later branching out into contemporary and classical theater. He is known for his exceptional talent in theater, particularly in Shakespearean plays, as well as his impressive work in film. Over the years, he has performed in numerous theater productions across the country, including classic Shakespearean plays such as Hamlet, Henry the VI, and Antony and Cleopatra among contemporary plays.

In addition to being an actor, Mateu is also a talented producer. He founded a theater company called the Community in Barcelona back in 2018, overseeing the production of several successful plays and improv theater. Some of his short films have been selected and nominated for prestigious awards, showcasing his versatility and creativity.



Mateu also worked as an assistant casting director at the Frankstien Studio in Barcelona, under the guidance of Luci Lennox. His experience in casting has given him a unique insight into the world of performance, enabling him to better understand the needs and requirements of actors and filmmakers alike. With his dedication to his craft and a passion for storytelling, Mateu continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of theater and film. He is committed to telling stories that resonate with audiences and showcasing diverse perspectives on the stage and screen. His exceptional talent, Mediterranean charm, and passion for the arts make him a force to be reckoned with in the entertainment industry.
